The story behind the app

Pet Care started with one kitten and a lot of questions.

Hello! I am Michelle Reyes, the creator of Pet Care. The inspiration for this website began when I welcomed Rocky, the kitten you see here, into my life.

I grew up with dogs, so feeding, training, and grooming felt familiar. Rocky changed that. Suddenly I was asking new questions about what kittens eat, how often they should be bathed, and what good day-to-day care really looks like.

That experience sparked the idea for Pet Care: a faster, friendlier way to get personalized pet care guidance without feeling overwhelmed or needing to start from scratch every time.
